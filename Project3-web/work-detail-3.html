Description

Vivienne Westwood's Spring-Summer 1997 "Vive la Bagatelle" collection masterfully blends historical references with playful modernity. Featuring designs inspired by 18th-century salon culture, the collection includes elements such as corsetry and intricate drapery, reimagined with contemporary fabrics and prints. This fusion showcases Westwood's signature approach of juxtaposing historical elegance with avant-garde innovation.
